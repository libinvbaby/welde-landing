About Us

Welde Engineering Solutions Pvt. Ltd., a pioneer and reputed manufacturers and dealers of steel doors, windows and frames with strong supply, installation & service base in Kerala. Our company's versatile product range comprises various doors like panel, windowed, sliding, storm, pocket, bypass and more. Our products are largely premium over the competitors because of its fine glossy shine that no other manufacturer can offer you as well as the quality and brand of input materials; we have gained a large client base for our establishment.
By completely avoiding the cold roll sheet we can assure you of the additional guarantee for all of our products. We will deliver the product at your site and install it properly with extra jobs like wall cutting and plastering. We are manufacturing our window frames and profiles in 100% TATA or other reputed steel brand galvanized iron so the qualities of the products are undisputable. We also customize the GI doors, windows, frames etc as per the requirements and taste of our customers
